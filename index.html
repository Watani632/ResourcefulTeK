About ResourcefulTeK

A resourceful partner for businesses that refuse to look ordinary

We started ResourcefulTeK because too many good businesses were being let down by generic branding and disconnected digital tools. We build the whole picture: identity, presence, marketing, and automation, so it all actually works together.

Our Story

Built for founders doing more with less

ResourcefulTeK was founded on a simple observation: most small and growing businesses in Nigeria and beyond don’t need more vendors. They need one team that understands how brand, web, marketing, and technology fit together.

The name is deliberate. We work resourcefully: efficient with time and budget, without cutting corners on quality. And we build with technology, including AI, as a growth tool rather than a buzzword.
